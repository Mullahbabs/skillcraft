Our Journey

History & Background

From a simple idea to a leading vocational training institution, our journey has been marked by innovation, dedication, and a commitment to transforming lives through practical education.

2015

The Beginning

Founded with a vision to bridge the skills gap in technical fields. Started with just two courses: Basic Welding and Electrical Installation.

2017

First Expansion

Moved to a larger facility and added four new programs including Solar Installation and Plumbing, responding to market demands.

2019

Accreditation Achieved

Received national accreditation and formed partnerships with industry leaders to enhance job placement for graduates.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched our online learning platform, allowing students to access theoretical components remotely while attending practical sessions.

2023

Milestone: 2,500 Graduates

Celebrated our 2,500th graduate with a 94% employment rate across all programs, solidifying our impact on the workforce.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical, high-demand skills through accelerated vocational training, creating pathways to meaningful employment and economic independence.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading vocational training institution, recognized for transforming lives and fueling economic growth through skills development and innovation.
